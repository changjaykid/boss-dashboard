### 4.2 具體參數優化建議

```python
# 現行 → 建議
CONFIDENCE_THRESHOLD = 0.75  →  0.80
MAX_TRADES_PER_DAY = None    →  10

    confidence_threshold += 0.10  # 高波動時更嚴格
```
