# Trend Following Strategies

## 1. EMA Cross Trend (EMA 交叉趨勢)

**Market regime**: TRENDING_UP / TRENDING_DOWN

**Entry (做多)**:
- EMA 9 > EMA 21 > EMA 50
- Price pulls back to EMA 21 (dynamic support)
- 15M candle shows bullish reversal pattern (hammer, engulfing)
- RSI > 50 (confirm bullish momentum)

**Entry (做空)**: Mirror conditions

**Exit**:
- TP: ATR × 3 or next S/R level
- SL: Below EMA 50 or ATR × 1.2 below entry
- Trailing: Move SL to breakeven after 1:1, trail by ATR × 1

**Best for**: GOLD, EUR/USD, GBP/USD during trending sessions

---

## 2. Multi-Timeframe Trend Alignment

**Market regime**: TRENDING_UP / TRENDING_DOWN

**Entry (做多)**:
- 4H: Price > EMA 50, making higher highs/lows
- 1H: Price > EMA 21, MACD histogram positive
- 15M: Bullish candlestick at EMA 9 or 21 support
- All three timeframes aligned bullish

**Entry (做空)**: Mirror conditions

**Exit**:
- TP: 4H S/R level or Fibonacci extension 1.618
- SL: Below 1H EMA 50
- Let winners ride if 4H trend intact

**Best for**: All instruments, strongest signals

---

## 3. Elliott Wave 3 Entry

**Market regime**: TRENDING (early stage)

**Entry**:
- Identify completed Wave 1 (impulse) + Wave 2 (correction)
- Wave 2 retraces to 50-61.8% Fibonacci of Wave 1
- Bullish reversal candle at Fibonacci support
- MACD crossing bullish
- Enter at start of Wave 3

**Exit**:
- TP: Wave 1 × 1.618 extension
- SL: Below Wave 1 start (invalidates count)

**Best for**: Catching the strongest wave in any trending market

---

## 4. Flag/Pennant Breakout

**Market regime**: TRENDING (after impulse)

**Entry**:
- Strong impulse move (pole)
- Price consolidates in flag/pennant pattern (3-10 bars)
- Breakout in pole direction with momentum
- Volume increase on breakout (if available)

**Exit**:
- TP: Measured move = pole height projected from breakout
- SL: Other side of flag/pennant

**Best for**: US indices after strong directional moves
