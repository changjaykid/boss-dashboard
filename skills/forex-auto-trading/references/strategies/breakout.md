# Breakout Strategies

## 1. Pattern Breakout (型態突破)

**Market regime**: BREAKOUT / post-RANGING

**Entry**:
- Identified chart pattern (triangle, rectangle, wedge, H&S neckline)
- Price breaks pattern boundary with strong candle (body > 70% of range)
- Volume increase on breakout (if available)
- Wait for retest of breakout level → enter on confirmation

**Exit**:
- TP: Measured move (pattern height projected from breakout)
- SL: Back inside the pattern (below breakout for long, above for short)
- If no retest within 3 candles, enter with half size

**Best for**: After Bollinger squeeze or extended consolidation

---

## 2. Bollinger Squeeze Breakout

**Market regime**: BREAKOUT

**Entry**:
- Bollinger Band width at 20-period low (squeeze)
- Bands start expanding
- Strong candle breaks out of bands
- MACD histogram confirms direction

**Exit**:
- TP: ATR × 4 (breakouts tend to extend)
- SL: Middle band (SMA 20) or ATR × 1.5
- Trail stop after 2:1 reached

**Best for**: All instruments, especially effective before London/NY open

---

## 3. Range Breakout (箱型突破)

**Market regime**: post-RANGING

**Entry**:
- Price has been in clear range for 10+ candles (1H)
- Breakout above resistance or below support
- Volume confirmation
- Preferably aligns with higher timeframe trend

**Exit**:
- TP: Range height projected from breakout
- SL: Opposite side of range or range midpoint
- Wider SL acceptable because breakout measured moves are large

**Best for**: After Asian session range → London breakout

---

## 4. Outside Bar Breakout

**Market regime**: BREAKOUT / VOLATILE

**Entry**:
- Outside bar (engulfs previous bar completely)
- Trade in direction of outside bar close
- Stronger if at S/R level or after consolidation

**Exit**:
- TP: ATR × 2.5
- SL: Opposite end of outside bar
- Quick decision — if not working within 3-5 bars, consider exiting

**Best for**: Volatile sessions, news-driven moves (after initial spike)
