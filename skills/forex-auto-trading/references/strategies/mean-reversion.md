# Mean Reversion & Reversal Strategies

## 1. S/R Reversal (支撐壓力反轉)

**Market regime**: RANGING

**Entry (做多)**:
- Price reaches strong support level (historical, psychological, or Fibonacci)
- Bullish reversal candlestick (hammer, morning star, bullish engulfing)
- RSI < 35 (oversold)
- Bollinger lower band touch or penetration
- Volume spike on reversal candle

**Entry (做空)**: Mirror at resistance

**Exit**:
- TP: Opposite S/R level or range midpoint
- SL: Below support by ATR × 0.5 (tight)
- Quick partial profit at 1:1

**Best for**: RANGING markets, especially during Asian session

---

## 2. RSI Divergence Reversal

**Market regime**: RANGING or late TRENDING

**Entry (做多)**:
- Price makes lower low
- RSI makes higher low (bullish divergence)
- Bullish candlestick confirmation
- Near support level

**Entry (做空)**:
- Price makes higher high
- RSI makes lower high (bearish divergence)
- Bearish candlestick confirmation
- Near resistance level

**Exit**:
- TP: Previous swing high/low
- SL: Beyond the divergence low/high

**Best for**: Catching trend exhaustion and reversals

---

## 3. Bollinger Band Mean Reversion

**Market regime**: RANGING

**Entry (做多)**:
- Price touches or penetrates lower Bollinger Band
- RSI < 30
- Closes back inside the bands
- Bullish candle on re-entry

**Entry (做空)**: Mirror at upper band

**Exit**:
- TP: Middle band (SMA 20) or opposite band
- SL: Below the candle that penetrated the band

**Best for**: Range-bound GOLD and forex pairs

---

## 4. Pin Bar at Key Level

**Market regime**: Any (strongest at RANGING)

**Entry**:
- Pin bar (long wick ≥ 2x body) at significant S/R level
- Pin bar wick rejects the level (shows buying/selling pressure)
- Confirmation from RSI or Stochastic

**Exit**:
- TP: Next S/R level
- SL: Beyond the pin bar wick

**Best for**: All instruments at major S/R levels
