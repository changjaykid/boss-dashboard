# Crypto Mean Reversion & Reversal Strategies

## 1. S/R Reversal

**Market regime**: RANGING

**Entry (做多)**:
- Price at strong support (historical, psychological like BTC $80K, $90K, $100K)
- Bullish reversal candle
- RSI < 35
- Bollinger lower band touch
- Fear & Greed < 30 (extreme fear = bonus confirmation)

**Exit**:
- TP: Opposite S/R or range midpoint
- SL: Below support by ATR × 0.5
- Quick partial at 1:1

---

## 2. RSI Divergence Reversal

**Market regime**: RANGING or late TRENDING

**Entry (做多)**:
- Price lower low + RSI higher low (bullish divergence)
- Bullish candle confirmation
- Near support level
- Volume declining on lower low (selling exhaustion)

**Exit**:
- TP: Previous swing high
- SL: Beyond divergence low

**Crypto note**: RSI divergence is very effective in crypto because of retail emotion — divergence catches exhaustion points well.

---

## 3. Bollinger Mean Reversion

**Market regime**: RANGING

**Entry (做多)**:
- Price touches/penetrates lower Bollinger Band
- RSI < 30
- Closes back inside bands
- Not during a liquidation cascade (check for abnormal volume)

**Exit**:
- TP: Middle band (SMA 20) or upper band
- SL: Below penetration candle

**Warning**: In crypto, Bollinger walks (price hugging the band) are more common than forex. If price stays outside band for 3+ candles, this isn't ranging — it's trending. Don't fight it.

---

## 4. Pin Bar at Key Level

**Market regime**: Any

**Entry**:
- Pin bar at significant S/R
- Long wick ≥ 2× body
- Wick rejects the level
- Confirmation from RSI or volume

**Exit**:
- TP: Next S/R
- SL: Beyond pin bar wick

---

## 5. Liquidation Bounce (幣圈特有)

**Market regime**: After VOLATILE dump

**Entry (做多)**:
- Sharp price drop (> 3% in < 1H) — likely liquidation cascade
- Long lower wick on 15M or 1H candle (buyers stepping in)
- Volume spike then declining
- RSI < 25 (extreme oversold)
- Price recovers above key support

**Exit**:
- TP: Pre-dump level or 50% Fibonacci retracement of the dump
- SL: Below the wick low (tight, because if it breaks, more liquidations coming)

**Why crypto-specific**: Liquidation cascades don't exist in forex CFDs. In crypto, leveraged positions get force-closed, creating waterfalls followed by sharp V-recoveries. Catching the bounce = high R:R.
