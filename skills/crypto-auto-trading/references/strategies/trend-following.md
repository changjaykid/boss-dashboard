# Crypto Trend Following Strategies

## 1. EMA Cross Trend

**Market regime**: TRENDING_UP / TRENDING_DOWN

**Entry (做多)**:
- EMA 8 > EMA 21 > EMA 50
- Price pulls back to EMA 21
- Bullish reversal candle (hammer, engulfing) on 15M
- RSI > 50, MACD histogram positive
- BTC direction aligned (don't long alts when BTC dumping)

**Entry (做空)**: Mirror conditions

**Exit**:
- TP: ATR × 2.5 or next S/R
- SL: ATR × 1.2 below entry (max 1.5%)
- Trailing: Lock breakeven at +0.5%, widen trail at +1% and +2%

---

## 2. Multi-Timeframe Trend Alignment

**Market regime**: TRENDING_UP / TRENDING_DOWN

**Entry (做多)**:
- 4H: Price > EMA 50, higher highs/lows
- 1H: Price > EMA 21, MACD positive
- 15M: Bullish candle at EMA support
- 5M: Optional fine-tune entry timing

**Exit**:
- TP: 4H resistance or Fibonacci 1.618 extension
- SL: Below 1H EMA 50
- Let winners ride — only exit when 4H trend breaks

**Best for**: BTC/ETH during strong directional moves

---

## 3. Momentum Chase (幣圈特有)

**Market regime**: TRENDING (strong) or BREAKOUT

**Entry**:
- Price breaks out with volume > 2× average
- RSI > 60 (for long) — momentum confirmed, NOT overbought in strong trend
- Fear & Greed Index NOT at extreme greed (< 80)
- Enter on first pullback after breakout (not at the peak)

**Exit**:
- TP: Let it run — trail by ATR × 1.5
- SL: Below breakout candle low
- Time exit: If momentum fades within 2H, close

**Why crypto-specific**: Crypto momentum is stronger than forex. When BTC moves, it MOVES. Catching momentum early with proper trailing = big R:R wins.

---

## 4. Elliott Wave 3 Entry

**Market regime**: TRENDING (early stage)

**Entry**:
- Completed Wave 1 + Wave 2 correction
- Wave 2 retraces 50-61.8% Fibonacci
- Bullish reversal at Fib support
- MACD crossing bullish

**Exit**:
- TP: Wave 1 × 1.618 extension
- SL: Below Wave 1 start

---

## 5. Flag/Pennant Breakout

**Market regime**: TRENDING (after impulse)

**Entry**:
- Strong impulse (pole) followed by consolidation (flag)
- Breakout in pole direction
- Volume increase on breakout

**Exit**:
- TP: Measured move = pole height from breakout
- SL: Other side of flag

**Best for**: BTC/ETH after news-driven spikes
