# Crypto Breakout Strategies

## 1. Pattern Breakout

**Market regime**: BREAKOUT / post-RANGING

**Entry**:
- Chart pattern identified (triangle, rectangle, wedge, H&S neckline)
- Breakout with strong candle (body > 70% of range)
- Volume > 1.5× average on breakout
- Wait for retest if possible

**Exit**:
- TP: Measured move (pattern height from breakout)
- SL: Back inside pattern

---

## 2. Bollinger Squeeze Breakout

**Market regime**: BREAKOUT

**Entry**:
- Bollinger bandwidth at period low (squeeze)
- Bands start expanding
- Strong candle breaks out
- MACD confirms direction

**Exit**:
- TP: ATR × 4 (breakouts extend far in crypto)
- SL: Middle band or ATR × 1.5
- Trail after 2:1

**Crypto note**: Bollinger squeezes in crypto often lead to explosive moves. The TP can be more aggressive than forex.

---

## 3. Range Breakout

**Market regime**: post-RANGING

**Entry**:
- Clear range for 20+ candles (15M) or 10+ candles (1H)
- Breakout above/below range
- Volume confirmation
- BTC direction aligned

**Exit**:
- TP: Range height projected from breakout
- SL: Range midpoint or opposite side

**Best for**: BTC/ETH during consolidation periods before big moves

---

## 4. Outside Bar Breakout

**Market regime**: BREAKOUT / VOLATILE

**Entry**:
- Outside bar engulfs previous bar
- Trade in close direction
- Stronger at S/R or after consolidation

**Exit**:
- TP: ATR × 2.5
- SL: Opposite end of outside bar

---

## 5. News-Driven Breakout (幣圈特有)

**Market regime**: Any (triggered by news)

**Entry**:
- Major news event (regulation, ETF, hack, partnership)
- Price gaps or spikes with extreme volume
- **Don't chase the initial spike**
- Wait for first pullback (usually 15-30 min after news)
- Enter if pullback holds above pre-news level (bullish) or below (bearish)

**Exit**:
- TP: Let it run with trailing stop (news moves can be 5-20%)
- SL: Below pullback low (tight)
- Time limit: If no continuation within 2H, close

**Why crypto-specific**: Crypto is far more news-driven than forex. A single regulatory announcement or hack can move the entire market. The key is NOT to chase — wait for the pullback, then ride the continuation.

### News sources to monitor
- CoinDesk, CoinTelegraph (mainstream)
- X/Twitter crypto accounts (real-time)
- Exchange announcements (listing/delisting = big moves)
- Regulatory news (SEC, CFTC, EU MiCA)
